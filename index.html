WHY SHOULD YOU BE PART OD THE GROOVEPET COMMUNITY?

Because you will find all the information about your pet in one place, you don't have to google 100 sites, call ten friends and read reviews that are set up. You are just a click away from the answer for any pet. Because the whole team is gathered around the love of pets, there are no haters who don't like animals, and no redundant comments, whether you are looking for a vet, a salon, an asylum, or just a friend to walk your pets with... you are in the right and safe place.

DONATION APPLICATION 100 %

The Donation application exists within each profile, and each user has the option to activate it in the settings. The GroovePet network will thus connect people who want to donate to other users via Paypal money transfer payment. The Donation option is the ability to voluntarily receive donations and a unique way that allows users to monetize their profile.

  • Friends

    Adding friends strengthens the connections inside your community. Members can add friends and accept or decline friend requests.
